Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence in Pool Care Since 1955

Well over 65 years of experience and a legacy spanning three generations, Nichols Pool Service has been a trusted name in pool care since 1955. Originally founded in Bristol, PA, the company is now proudly owned and operated by Paul "The Pool Guy" Cramer, continuing the family tradition of excellence.

All our technicians are Certified Pool Operators (CPO) certified, ensuring every job meets the highest standards of quality and safety. With expertise spanning decades, we combine knowledge and dedication to deliver exceptional service to pool owners across the region.
